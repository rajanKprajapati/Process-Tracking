## Super Fast Industry Trick

When you see a table ask:

Question 1

Is there Month, Year, Date, Quarter?

Yes?

➡ Time Series

➡ Line Chart

Question 2

Is there Product, Department, City, Employee, Country?

Yes?

➡ Categorical

➡ Bar Chart

Question 3

Are values percentages or parts of a total?

Yes?

➡ Composition

➡ Pie Chart
